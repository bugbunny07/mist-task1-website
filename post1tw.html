My First Blog Post

Posted on September 27, 2025

Welcome to my very first blog post! I started this blog as a way to document my journey into web development and share what I learn along the way. I’ve always been fascinated by how websites work, and now I’m finally learning to build my own.

Today, I wrote my first HTML page! I learned about tags like <h1> for headings, <p> for paragraphs, and <a> for links. I even added some basic CSS to style my text and background.

It feels amazing to see something I wrote appear on a real webpage. I know I still have a long way to go, but every small step feels like progress.

Next, I plan to explore more about responsive design and layouts. I can’t wait to make my site look good on phones and laptops too!
